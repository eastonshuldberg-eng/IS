Delicious Green Bean Recipes

Simple • Fresh • Flavorful

purple Garlic Parmesan Green Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh green beans
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Trim ends off green beans.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic for 1 minute.
  3. Add green beans and cook 6–8 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Serve warm.

Honey Soy Green Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 lb green beans
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Steam green beans until tender-crisp.
  2. Mix soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil.
  3. Toss beans in sauce and heat 2 minutes.
  4. Top with sesame seeds and serve.

Green Bean Almondine

Ingredients

  • 1 lb green beans
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil green beans until bright and tender.
  2. Toast almonds in butter until golden.
  3. Add green beans and lemon juice.
  4. Toss, season, and serve.
